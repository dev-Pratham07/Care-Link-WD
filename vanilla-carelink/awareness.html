Vaccination Schedule

Stay protected with timely vaccinations:

  • • Annual flu vaccine (recommended for everyone)
  • • COVID-19 vaccine and boosters
  • • Tetanus booster every 10 years
  • • Age-specific vaccines (consult your doctor)

Emergency Preparedness

Be prepared for health emergencies:

  • • Keep a first aid kit at home and in your car
  • • Know emergency contact numbers
  • • Learn basic CPR and first aid
  • • Maintain a list of your medications and allergies

Prevention is Better Than Cure

By following these simple guidelines, you can significantly reduce your risk of illness and improve your quality of life.

Start with small changes today for a healthier tomorrow!
